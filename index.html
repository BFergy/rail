About this site

This site is NOT mobile friendly! It may work (sort of) in landscape mode, depending on your device.

Back in the dark ages, I created several web sites that were considered pretty good at the time. But well over 25 years have passed since then. This is merely a clone of an old site I built as I have no interest in learning newer web development methods.

In essence, I authored these pages while waiting for paint or glue to dry, or scenery materials to harden on my layout. If you're looking for entertainment there are far better places to find it!

But if you're bored, the destination links at the left will transport you to pages where I demonstrate my lack of skill and knowledge.
